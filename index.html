Recipe #1

  • Time: 7:30 am
  • Serves: Pancake
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 100g
egg 2 pcs
melted butter 2 spoon
milk 1 cup

Procedures:

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth.

Step 2:Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 7:30 am
  • Serves: Ham and scrumble egg
Ingredients Quantity
Egg beaten 8 pcs
milk 2 tablespoon
salt and paper 1/4 table spoon
Ham 20g

Procedures:

Step 1: Beat eggs, milk, seasoned salt, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.

Step 2:Pour egg mixture into ham mixture. Cook and stir until eggs are set but not dry, 3 to 5 minutes. Sprinkle 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese over eggs; cook and stir until cheese is melted. Transfer eggs to a plate and sprinkle remaining cheese over the top.
